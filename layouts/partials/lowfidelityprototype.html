Low-Fidelity Prototype

Following the user stories, I created a low-fidelity prototype in Marvel. The product was starting to develop, but it needed to be tested. A survey was created to find 5 appropriate candidates for testing the prototype. 

The candidates must have an interest in gardening, they must reside in the US, and they must be of an adult age and considered independent. The latter question was determined by asking about their living situation and their age. 

The candidates were chosen and the interviews were conducted via Zoom or in-person. The candidates gave valuable feedback at this stage of development. I proceeded by creating wire flows using the prototype and made changes based on the information I had gathered from the interviews.
