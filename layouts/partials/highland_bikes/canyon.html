Canyon

Canyon's checkout process stood out to me for its omission of account requirements, simplifying the process initially. However, this could pose a challenge for returning customers who rely on saving their information. Despite this, the process remained straightforward. The layout appeared more modern compared to REI's.

As for the comparison feature, while initially user-friendly with clear labeling, the limited 3-product maximum became problematic due to the need to rotate my phone to fit all information, leading to longer load times and awkward navigation. Nonetheless, the ability to collapse and expand categories successfully reduced my cognitive load. Despite the clunkiness of the comparison page, Canyon's overall design felt modern and met accessibility standards, with contrasting colors and rounded corners contributing to a more approachable appearance.
