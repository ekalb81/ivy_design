REI

In my assessment of the REI eCommerce app, I found both strengths and areas for improvement. The checkout process impressed me with its simplicity, offering a guest checkout option to bypass mandatory account creation. However, concerns arose regarding the visibility of the comparison feature, which might be overlooked due to its small size and unclear CTA. Despite this, once engaged, the comparison tool proved useful, allowing for clear and easy comparison of up to five bikes. Yet, limitations arose when comparing more than two bikes, requiring inconvenient horizontal scrolling. Despite its plain design, the app prioritized readability and accessibility, though there's room for enhancement in striking a balance between simplicity and engagement to elevate the overall user experience.
