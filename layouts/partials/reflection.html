Reflection & Next Steps

I learned a lot through the development of Seed & Soil. For one thing, it’s impossible to make too many iterations of a prototype because problems will always arise. However, it’s important to keep in mind that the goal of creating any product is to improve someone’s life in some way. As long as a product can accomplish this, with as few bugs as possible, then I would consider that a success. 

The next steps for Seed & Soil will be teaming up with software developers in order to start building the back end of this product. That will mean more testing with users, and likely more design changes and multiple prototypes! I’m looking forward to the future of this product.
