Defining

Once I created the personas and empathy maps, I used them as a guide to create “How Might We” statements. This led to 4 important aspects that the product needed to address.

How might we motivate full-time workers to make time for gardening?

How might we make gardening information more accessible and digestible for novice gardeners?

How might we give experienced gardeners more resources and information they could use?

How might we help users find the right physical gardening tools at the best possible prices?
