Personas

Using the insights that I gained, I created 2 personas. Both of them differ in age and gardening experience. This will help ensure that the product is more accessible. 

Mandy – A younger, full-time working single woman. She lives in an apartment and wants to become more self-sufficient, but she feels overwhelmed with work.

Derrick – An older, more financially established, and experienced gardener. He wants to garden as a hobby for his well-being and as a way to spend time with his family. He has trouble grasping new technology.
