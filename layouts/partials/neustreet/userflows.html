User Flows

I had initially created two user flows. The first flow captured the path of a user who worked for a larger corporation looking to access our services for their company. It shows the user accessing a sample of information that Neustreet will provide and progresses to the user contacting us about our product.

The second flow was meant to capture the path that a developer would take to access our API, a page that has for now been put on hold. However, I have still included the user flow as it was still part of the design process.
