Information Architecture

Before diving into the low-fidelity prototypes, I created a very bare-bones site map that would reflect the consolidation of the current content on the Neustreet website. The navigation had several categories that were redundant. For instance, the Ideas, Learn, and Insights categories could have all been unified, as well as the Newsletters and Podcasts categories. However, because the team decided that they wanted to move away from having so much Media content on their website, some of these categories were removed completely.
