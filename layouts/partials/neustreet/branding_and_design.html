Branding & Design

We kept the logo from before, and we incorporated the primary colors of the logo into the rest of the website. The hot pink was used for the logo, CTAs, and other small accents. Along with the primary colors of hot pink and black, we incorporated pinks and purples into the background. They wanted to have a cyberpunk feel while still maintaining a professional look. 

The previous website had a lot of images that were inspired by graffiti, but we moved away from this imagery in favor of cyberpunk and data-like imagery. I felt that this gave the user a sense of trust, similar to the imagery used in the Azure.microsoft page that I reviewed in the competitive analysis.

Font

The site formerly used Montserrat, but upon further investigation, I discovered that many people have a difficult time reading that font due to dyslexia or vision impairment. We opted to change the site-wide font to Roboto because it reflected our product and was more readable.
