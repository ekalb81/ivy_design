Insights

I started this project on the basis that gardening could help cut the cost of grocery bills, but I quickly found a wealth of reasons why people want to garden. From the interviews, I gathered 3 valuable insights that could help shape the future of this project. 

The sources interviewees used for gathering data were consistent with quantitative research. However, apps were considered too cumbersome or not used at all. 

All participants shared a feeling of nostalgia associated with gardening with a relative, such as a parent or grandparent.

The younger demographic encounters more obstacles to gardening.
